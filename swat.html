Swat Valley

Explore lush green meadows, crystal alpine rivers, and historical Buddhist archaeological paths.

Key Expeditions

Essential geographic and historic interest zones within the Swat valley alpine system.

Kalam Upper Valley

An enchanting upper-altitude alpine basin surrounded by dense pine forests, majestic glacial waters, and the pristine origin points of the Swat River.

Malam Jabba Ski Resort

Pakistan's premier high-altitude winter ski base, presenting sweeping panoramic snowfields, cable car lift rides, and lush summer trekking lines.

Mahodand Glacial Lake

A breathtaking, high-altitude pristine water reservoir accessed via off-road rugged paths, dotted with wildflower alpine fields and heavy cascades.
