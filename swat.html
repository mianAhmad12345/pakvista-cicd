About Swat Valley

Swat Valley is one of the most beautiful regions of Pakistan. Located in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, it is famous for green valleys, rivers, forests and snow-covered mountains. Because of its natural beauty, it is known as the Switzerland of Pakistan.

Malam Jabba

Malam Jabba is Pakistan's leading ski resort. Visitors enjoy skiing, chair lifts, zip lines and mountain views. It is a major tourist attraction during both summer and winter seasons.

Mingora Bazaar

Mingora Bazaar is the commercial center of Swat Valley. Tourists can purchase handicrafts, gemstones, traditional dresses and local products while exploring the culture of the region.

Swat Museum

Swat Museum contains ancient Buddhist sculptures, archaeological findings, coins and historical artifacts. It helps visitors understand the rich history and culture of the valley.
