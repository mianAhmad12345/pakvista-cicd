About Swat Valley

Swat Valley is one of the most beautiful regions of Pakistan. Located in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, it is famous for green valleys, rivers, forests and snow-covered mountains. Because of its natural beauty, it is known as the Switzerland of Pakistan.
