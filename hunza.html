Hunza Portfolio

Framed by towering Rakaposhi and Ultar Sar peaks, Hunza offers pristine alpine lakes, terraced orchards, and centuries-old fortresses.

Key Expeditions

Essential geographic and historic interest zones within the Hunza alpine valley ecosystem.

Attabad Turquoise Lake

Born from a massive landslide in 2010, this deep turquoise body of water offers spectacular boat tours amidst sheer, jagged mountain walls.

Ancient Baltit Fort

A majestic 700-year-old architectural landmark overlooking Karimabad, reflecting heavy Tibetan structural influences and royal histories.

Passu Cathedral Cones

The iconic, needle-sharp mountain ridges standing tall along the Karakoram Highway, offering unparalleled panoramic photography vistas.
