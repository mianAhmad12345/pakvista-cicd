About Hunza Valley

Hunza is a mountainous valley in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan. It is situated in the extreme northern part of the country, bordering with the Wakhan Corridor of Afghanistan and the Xinjiang region of China. Known for its stunning scenery and longevity of its inhabitants, it's often referred to as Heaven on Earth.

Baltit Fort

Baltit Fort is an ancient fort in the Hunza valley in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. Founded in the 8th CE, it has been on the UNESCO World Heritage Tentative list since 2004.

Attabad Lake

Attabad Lake is a lake in the Gojal Valley of northern Pakistan that was created in January 2010 by a landslide dam. Its bright blue colors make it a top tourist destination.
