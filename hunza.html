About Hunza Valley

Hunza Valley is one of the most beautiful tourist destinations in Pakistan. It is located in Gilgit-Baltistan and is famous for its mountains, lakes, pleasant weather, and historical places.

Popular Tourist Attractions

Rakaposhi View Point

Rakaposhi View Point offers an amazing view of the snow-covered Rakaposhi mountain and is a favorite stop for tourists.

Attabad Lake

Attabad Lake is famous for its crystal-clear blue water and boating activities. It was formed after a landslide in 2010.

Baltit Fort

Baltit Fort is a historic fort in Karimabad that reflects the rich culture and history of Hunza Valley.
