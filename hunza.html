Hunza Valley

A mesmerizing alpine paradise framed by the Karakoram range, home to legendary hospitality and timeless historical citadels.

Key Expeditions

Essential points of cultural and geographic interest in the northern domain.

Altit & Baltit Forts

Architectural marvels dating back over 700 years, standing as guardians of the Silk Road legacy with panoramic valley views. Founded in the 8th CE, they remain landmarks on the UNESCO tented lists.

Attabad Lake

A striking, deep-blue body of water formed amidst dramatic canyon walls by a landslide dam in 2010. Ideal for boat expeditions and alpine photography.

Passu Cones

The serrated, knife-edge peaks of the Passu massif offering one of the most iconic vistas anywhere along the Karakoram Highway ecosystem.
