About Karachi

Karachi is the largest city of Pakistan and the capital of Sindh. Known as the "City of Lights", it is famous for its beaches, historical landmarks, museums, and vibrant culture. Karachi is the country's financial and commercial center.

Clifton Beach

Clifton Beach is one of Karachi's most visited tourist attractions. Visitors enjoy horse riding, camel rides, seaside views, and family-friendly activities along the coast.

Mohatta Palace

Mohatta Palace is a beautiful historical building constructed in 1927. Today it serves as a museum showcasing Pakistan's cultural heritage, art exhibitions, and historical collections.

Pakistan Maritime Museum

The Pakistan Maritime Museum presents the history of Pakistan's naval forces and maritime heritage. Visitors can explore naval equipment, submarines, and educational exhibits.
