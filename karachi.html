Key Locations

A sweeping, vibrant marine shoreline offering a wide open vista of the Arabian Sea, featuring active local markets, camel rides, and dynamic open retail nodes.

The pure white marble monument honoring the nation's founder, reflecting iconic modernist geometric silhouettes blended with traditional architecture.
