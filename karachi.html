About Karachi

Karachi is the largest city of Pakistan and the capital of Sindh province. It is known as the “City of Lights” due to its vibrant lifestyle, nightlife, and economic importance.

It is Pakistan’s main financial hub, home to ports, industries, business centers, and diverse cultures from across the country.

Top Attractions in Karachi

Clifton Beach

Clifton Beach is one of the most popular tourist spots in Karachi, offering camel rides, horse rides, street food, and sunset views.

Mohatta Palace

Mohatta Palace is a historic building built in 1927, now serving as a museum for art exhibitions and cultural heritage.

Pakistan Maritime Museum

This museum showcases Pakistan Navy history, submarines, and maritime artifacts.
